Semantic Tags

What are Semantic Tags?

Semantic tags clearly describe their purpose to both people reading your HTML code, and the browser that read and display it as a webpage.

Examples of semantic tags include: <header>, <main>, <section>, <nav>, <footer>, <img>, <table> and <article>.

Examples of non-semantic tags include: <div> and <span>.
