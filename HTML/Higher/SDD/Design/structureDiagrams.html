Structure Diagrams at Higher Level

Much like pseudocode, structure diagrams now also have to top level design that shows data flow. Below you can see a structure diagram for the same program from the pseudocode page. As before, the purpose of this program is to read the name, prelim mark and coursework mark for 20 pupils from a file.

Top Level Design and Data Flow

In a structure diagram, the data flow is shown by arrows in the top level design, arrows pointing down are outputs from a subprogram or subroutine and arrows pointing up are inputs.

Refinements

The subprograms shown in the Top Level Design will be broken down into smaller steps. They can be shown separately from the Top Level Design as they are individual subprograms.
