Analysis

During the analysis stage of website development, the following requirements should be identified:

End-user requirements:
  • The end users are the people who are going to be using the website
  • Their requirements are the tasks they expect to be able to do using the website
Functional requirements:
  • Processes and activities that the system has to perform
  • Information that the system has to contain to be able to carry out its functions

These requirements will help:

  • clarify the design of each webpage.
  • identify the features to be implemented on the website.
  • evaluate whether the system is fit for purpose after development is complete.

Example of End-User and Functional Requirements

A band wants to develop a website for its fans. The site will provide details about the band, including biographies, music tracks, video clips and concert details.

End-user requirements

Users should be able to...

  • navigate the site easily.
  • view biographies and photos of band members.
  • view all upcoming concerts and link to an external booking site.
  • view video clips of the band.
  • listen to the band’s audio tracks.

Functional requirements

The website must contain...

  • a home page that provides internal links to the topic pages (biographies, music, videos and concerts).
  • individual profile pages that include biography information, with photos of the band member and a link back to the biographies page.
  • a music page that lists the band's albums and allows individual audio tracks to be played.
  • a videos page that lists video clips and allows these to be played.
  • a concerts page listing the upcoming concerts with links to an external booking site.
  • a link on all of the pages that leads back to the home page.

Past Paper Question - 2023 Q19(a)

The Vulpes fox charity would like a website to increase public knowledge of foxes.

(a)

The following content is required.

The website will have pages about different types of foxes where users can view pictures and listen to the sounds made by each fox. The charity logo needs to be visible at the top of each page

Identify a functional requirement of the above website.

 1

One mark for any one from:

The website must...

  • display the charity logo.
  • play sounds of foxes.
  • display pictures of foxes.
